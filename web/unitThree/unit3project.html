Part 1 | DUE: before class on Thursday, October 22

For part 1 of Project 3, please observe the following obstructions.

  • Find an artwork / artist that incorporates the element / concept of Time in a substantial or meaningful way.
  • Find an artwork / artist that incorporates the element / concept of Change in a substantial or meaningful way.
  • Share these images along with a sentence or two on how you see those elements at play in the selected works.

Part 2 | DUE: Monday, November 9th, by 11:59pm

For part 2 of Project 3, please consider the skills we have covered in class thus far while observing the following obstructions

  • Create an original artwork using P5.js that incorporates both Time and Change as central or important elements in the work.
  • Your artwork must include an element of interactivity. Note: there are a variety of interesting ways to incorporate "outside" influence in your work in addition to the mouse and keyboard.
  • Your project can make use of found imagery, but any images incorporated into the experience must be recontextualized or altered to become part of your "original" piece.
  • If you choose to use text, you are limited to no more than three words total. This includes repetition of the same word.
  • The Craft of Creative Coding should be considered, meaning that your code should be well commented and well organized.
  • You must include a brief statement in the comments of your code. This statement should address how Time and Change were considered for your project.
  • To turn in your project, please share a link to the final piece in the slack channel #fa20-project-3.
