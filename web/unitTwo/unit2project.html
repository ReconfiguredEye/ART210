Unit 2 | Project 02

Part 1

For the first part of Project 2 I would like you to find a work of art (or perhaps an artist) that incorporates concepts centered on Body and Identity. There are lots of ways to make this argument, so long as you find the work fascinating and see these connections, then I'm on board. You should share your Artwork or Artist in the #fa20-project-2 Slack Channel with a paragraph describing what about the work that you find valuable along with how a / the value of the work might be established. This should be posted no later than Noon, Monday, September 28th, 2020. Please make sure your links to the artwork are in good working order. Be prepared to discuss these in class on Tuesday, September 29th.
